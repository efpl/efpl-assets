2) Squads

3) Matches

  • Conditions: Random.
  • Quitting mid-game = automatic loss.
  • If the connection is poor, pause early and agree to restart.
  • If possible, record or stream your games.

4) Fair Play

  • No Smart-Assist Very important
  • Respect opponents.
  • No time-wasting or toxic behaviour.
  • Pause etiquette: only at stoppages or with mutual consent.
  • No exploiting disconnects/lag; restart by agreement if needed.
  • Aim for competitive and enjoyable matches.

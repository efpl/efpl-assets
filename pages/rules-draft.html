Quick-copy rules

1) Formations Very important

Use only default formations from the in-game list. You may change the role label (Triangle/Y) but not the on-screen position.

  • No custom formations.
  • No dragging/moving player dots on the tactics board.
  • Allowed role swaps (slot stays put): CF ↔ SS, LWF ↔ SS, RWF ↔ SS, AMF ↔ CMF, DMF ↔ CMF
Example screenshots (formation screen)

2) Squads

  • No Big Time, Epics, or Showtime players.
  • Only Standard, Featured, Highlight, Trending players are allowed.
  • Sub Tactic must be disabled.
Where to find “Sub Tactic” toggle

3) Managers & Boosters

  • No Double Booster managers.
Required screenshots (pre-match)

4) Match Settings

Use these exact settings for all eFPL fixtures.

  • Match Length: 10 minutes
  • Player Condition: Random
  • Injuries: On (if applicable)
  • Extra Time / Pens: League: Off • Cup: As specified
  • Squad Numbering & Kits: Home chooses (avoid clashes)
  • Pauses: Stoppages only or by mutual consent
Match lobby screenshots

5) Fair Play

  • No Smart-Assist.
  • Respect opponents; no time-wasting or toxicity.
  • If connection is poor, pause early and agree a restart.
  • Record or stream matches when possible.

6) Proof & Disputes

Simple evidence → faster decisions.

  1. Pre-match: Home player posts manager screen + squad list (Booster icons visible) in the fixture thread/Discord.
  2. Post-match: Either player may be asked for the same screenshots if a dispute is raised.
  3. Video: Short clips of illegal formations/pauses speed up resolutions.

7) Penalties

  • First offence: match void & replay; formal warning.
  • Second offence: 0–3 forfeit.
  • Repeat: points deduction or removal from competition.

FAQ

What counts as a Double Booster manager?
Any manager whose effects stack to provide two booster-type benefits (or Link-Up/stacked traits) beyond standard managers. These are not allowed.
Can I use Featured/Trending players?
Yes — as long as they are not Big Time/Epics/Showtime (those are banned).
What if my opponent refuses screenshots?
Flag a mod immediately in the fixture thread; the match may be voided pending proof.

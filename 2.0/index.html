What is eFPL?

Fair play rules

No legends, epics, or showtime cards. A consistent ruleset that rewards skill.

Structured competition

Season leagues, fixtures, standings, and clear qualification for cups.

Community driven

Friendlies, pop tournaments, co-op nights, and active admin support.

Competitions

Leagues

Diamond, Ruby, Sapphire, and Crystal leagues with live standings and fixtures.

Cups

Champions Cup, Continental Cup, Conference Cup, and Horizon Cup.

Stats & history

Full stats breakdown and a permanent Hall of Fame.

What is eFPL?

eFPL is a structured eFootball league focused on fair competition and authentic football.

Fair play rules

No legends, epics, or showtime cards. Skill decides matches.

Structured leagues

Fixtures, standings, and promotion paths that make sense.

Community driven

Friendlies, pop tournaments, and active admin support.

Leagues

Multiple tiers designed to keep competition balanced.

Cups

Knockout tournaments with real pressure.

Hall of Fame

Every champion recorded, season by season.

eFPL is a community-run league and is not affiliated with Konami.
